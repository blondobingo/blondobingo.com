How to Play Bingo

Bingo is played in sessions. You purchase cards, numbers are called out and the first person to fill a winning pattern and shout “Bingo!” is the winner.

Most people are familiar with the five-numbers-in-a-row Bingo, but at Sunset there are at least SEVEN ways to hit the jackpot. Since every game has a free space, you only need four numbers to create a winning pattern:

  • Vertical line
  • Horizontal line
  • Diagonal line
  • Four Outside Corners
  • Top V’s
  • Bottom V’s

Getting Started

Arrive at least 30 minutes before your session starts. Sessions usually last for about 2 1/2 hours. Games can be played with paper cards or electronic machines.

Purchase your cards and find a seat in any section of the hall and get ready for us to begin calling numbers.

YELL “BINGO” LOUDLY when you have a bingo, but be sure to wait until the number has been verbally called out, not just when it’s visible on the monitor.

Your packets are valid for all of our regular games. During a bingo session, 2 special games are also played, such as Early Bird and Triangle.
