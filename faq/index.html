Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?

Play for as little as $10! Yes, ten dollars.

Who may play Bingo?

Adults at least 18 years old. Players must provide valid photo identification.

Do I have to play the whole session?

Yes, you must play the whole session.

Are daubers required for paper-card Bingo?

Yes, you must use daubers.

May I bring my own food and drinks?

Yes, you are allowed to bring in your own food, snacks and NON-ALCOHOLIC beverages to enjoy. Please do not bring alcohol of any kind into the facility or you may be asked to leave without any refunds.

What about “Parties” or “Special Events?”

Sure, we love to see celebrations at Blondo Bingo Omaha! Please let us know in advance so we can have everything ready. You are welcome to take over a table (or section of tables), decorate and bring in cake and refreshments. Your group will have a great time and perhaps win a few Bingos! Celebrations must be organized by patrons, we don’t reserve tables or have an event-planner. There are usually many free spots and entire tables available. Contact us if you have a large group (over 20) or have any questions.

Have another question?

Our staff is always glad to help. Or ask someone sitting nearby; bingo players are some of the friendliest people around!
